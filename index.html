GOT QUESTIONS?

Are your products authentic? +

Every single item — sneakers, vintage tees, hats, everything — is 100% authentic. We hand-pick every piece ourselves. No fakes. No replicas. Not here.

Where exactly are you? +

302 Park Central East, Downtown Springfield MO — right on Historic Route 66. We're 16 doors from the Route 66 Welcome Center. You'll see our graffiti sign in the window.

Can I try things on before buying? +

Yes — come in during store hours and try before you buy. That's the move. Especially for sneakers. We'd rather you love what you leave with.

Is local pickup really free? +

Completely free. Order online at jamiesshoess.squarespace.com, select local pickup at checkout, and swing by during store hours (Wed–Sat) to grab it.

Do you buy or trade? +

Come in and talk to us. We're always on the lookout for heat. Bring what you've got during store hours.

How often does new inventory drop? +

Weekly. Sometimes more. Follow @JAMIESSHOESS on Instagram to be first — we post every drop there before anything goes on the site.
