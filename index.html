Our Story

Why JAMIESSHOESS
Exists

Jamie built this from a real obsession with sneakers and vintage culture in Springfield, MO. What started as hunting for rare pieces turned into a full storefront right on Historic Route 66.

Every item in the store is hand-picked. No fakes. No replicas. No mass-bought bulk inventory. Just heat — curated for people who actually care about what they wear.

Come in, dig through the racks, find something nobody else has. That’s the whole point.

Got Questions

Quick Answers

Are your products authentic?
Every single item — sneakers, vintage tees, hats, everything — is 100% authentic. We hand-pick every piece ourselves. No fakes. No replicas. Not here.
Where exactly are you?
302 Park Central East, Downtown Springfield MO — right on Historic Route 66. We’re 16 doors from the Route 66 Welcome Center. You’ll see our graffiti sign in the window.
Can I try things on before buying?
Yes — come in during store hours and try before you buy. That’s the move. Especially for sneakers. We’d rather you love what you leave with.
Is local pickup really free?
Completely free. Order online, select local pickup, and swing by during store hours (Wed–Sat) to grab it.
Do you buy or trade?
Come in and talk to us. We’re always on the lookout for heat. Bring what you’ve got during store hours.
How often does new inventory drop?
Weekly. Sometimes more. Follow @JAMIESSHOESS on Instagram to be first — we post every drop there before anything goes anywhere else.
