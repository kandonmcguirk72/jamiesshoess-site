In a world of opportunity, it's your obligation to take action and create something for yourself.
— Jamie · Founder, JAMIESSHOESS

JAMIESSHOESS wasn't built from a business plan — it was built from a genuine obsession with finding things that matter. Rare sneakers, vintage tees with real stories, pieces that mass retail will never produce again.

What started as a personal passion became something bigger: a curated space in the heart of downtown Springfield where sneakerheads, vintage enthusiasts, and anyone looking for something different can find exactly that.

Every single item in the store has been hand-selected. That means no filler, no low-effort inventory. Just pieces worth owning — at prices that make sense for the community we serve.

We're also deeply committed to sustainability. The fashion industry is one of the biggest polluters on the planet, and every vintage purchase is a direct action against that. When you shop JAMIESSHOESS, you're not just getting something unique — you're making a choice that matters.

We're at 302 Park Central East, Wednesday through Saturday. Come in. Tell us what you're looking for. We'll find it.

The Journey
How We Got Here
Start

The Obsession Begins

Like most things that become real businesses, it started with a personal obsession — finding rare sneakers and vintage pieces that mainstream retail simply doesn't carry. Every find felt like a win.

Next

From Side Hustle to Storefront

What began as personal collecting turned into reselling, then into a real community around curated vintage. The move to 302 Park Central East in downtown Springfield made it official.

Today

Springfield's Vintage Authority

Now with 6,000+ Instagram followers, a 100% recommendation rating, and a loyal local community, JAMIESSHOESS is the destination for anyone in Springfield who wants something real.

Next

Growing the Vision

More drops. More categories. More ways to engage with the community. The mission is the same — find the best, share it with Springfield, and keep the vintage culture alive.
