Vintage & Authenticity
Every piece we carry is verified before listing. We look at construction (stitching, tags, hardware), materials, and provenance. We do not carry replicas or fakes — ever. If there's any doubt about an item, we don't list it.
We describe condition honestly in every listing. Common vintage grades: Excellent (like new or minimal wear), Good (normal vintage wear, all details intact), Fair (visible wear, still fully wearable). We call out any flaws — holes, stains, major fading — directly in the product description.
Vintage sizing runs differently from modern sizing — often smaller. A vintage "Large" might fit like a modern "Medium." We list the tag size as well as measurements where available. When in doubt, DM us for measurements of a specific piece before you buy.
Yes. We authenticate every pair — checking stitching, tag placement, box labels, sole construction, and overall quality. We're sneakerheads ourselves, and we've been doing this long enough to spot a fake. No replicas. Period.
